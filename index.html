About Us

At DVM Roofing and Construction, we’re more than just a roofing and construction company—we’re your trusted partner in protecting and improving your home or business. With years of hands-on experience, our team is committed to delivering high-quality workmanship, reliable service, and lasting results. From roof installations and repairs to siding, gutters, and storm restoration, we handle every project with care and precision. We take pride in using top-grade materials and ensuring every job is done right the first time. Whether you’re planning a new build, fixing storm damage, or upgrading your property’s exterior, we’re here to make the process smooth, stress-free, and successful. Your satisfaction is our priority. Let’s build something strong—together.

Our Services

  • Roof Installation & Replacement

    Complete installation of new roofs or full replacement of old, damaged ones using materials like asphalt shingles, metal, or tile.

  • Roof Repair & Maintenance

    Fixing leaks, damaged shingles, or flashing, and providing regular inspections to extend roof life and prevent costly issues.

  • Gutter Installation & Repair

    Installing or repairing gutters and downspouts to ensure proper water drainage and protect foundations and siding from water damage.

  • Siding Installation & Repair

    Enhancing exterior walls with durable siding materials (vinyl, fiber cement, wood) for better insulation, protection, and curb appeal.

  • Skylight & Vent Installation

    Adding skylights or roof vents to improve natural lighting and attic ventilation, boosting energy efficiency and comfort.

  • Storm Damage Restoration

    Emergency repairs and insurance assistance after hail, wind, or storm damage to quickly restore structural integrity and safety.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if my roof needs to be replaced or just repaired?

We recommend a professional inspection to assess the extent of the damage. Minor issues like a few missing shingles can be repaired, but widespread damage or aging may require a full replacement.

How long does a roof replacement usually take?

Most residential roof replacements are completed within 1 to 3 days, depending on the size and complexity of the project.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes, we provide free, no-obligation estimates for all roofing and construction services.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. We are fully licensed, bonded, and insured to ensure your peace of mind throughout the project.

What kind of roofing materials do you use?

We work with a variety of materials including asphalt shingles, metal roofing, clay tiles, and more—based on your budget and aesthetic preferences.

Can you help with insurance claims for storm damage?

Yes, we assist clients with the insurance claims process to help make repairs or replacements as smooth and stress-free as possible.
